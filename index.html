Get to know me

My story isn't a particularly exciting one, but it's led to who I am today, and I'm pretty proud of that. I didn't always know I'd be a developer, in fact if someone had asked me 5 years ago what one even did I wouldn't have been able to give an accurate answer. I'm so early in my development as a developer but despite my short time in the field I feel like I've learned an incredible amount and tapped into a passion I didn't think I'd ever have. As I grow as a developer I'm excited to continuously update this portfolio. It will not only be a way for me to demonstrate my aquired skills in the field and allow readers to get a sense of my personality but a way to measure my own growth. Thanks for stopping by my portfolio, I hope that you learned about who I am as a developer and person!
